Login tokens are deprecated

Effective July 14, 2023, login tokens have been retired and replaced with our new authentication system, yAuth. To proceed, create an account or log in and visit the site's direct URL.

Notably, the Quotes library collection can now be directly accessed at quotes.yeahgames.net. Please note that access to the Quotes library requires you to be logged in with an authorized yEAh account.

If you encounter any dead links leading to a login token, please contact us by emailing accounts+lt@yeahgames.net.
